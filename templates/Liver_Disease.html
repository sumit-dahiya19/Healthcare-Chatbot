Liver Disease

Liver disease is any disturbance of liver function that causes illness. The liver is responsible for many critical functions within the body and should it become diseased or injured, the loss of those functions can cause significant damage to the body
Symptoms:Symptoms vary but may include yellow eyes and skin (jaundice), nausea, abdominal pain and swelling, and swelling of the legs
Treatments:Treatment depends on the type of liver disease and can include taking medication and avoiding alcohol. A transplant may be required in advanced cases.
